### Institution: University of Tuscia
**Timeline:** May 2025

#### Overview
This structural case study investigates the modernization of the electrical grid within post-conflict regional configurations, focusing directly on Mogadishu's utility architecture.

#### Strategic Implementations
* **Utility Assessment:** Used the operations data of the **Banadir Electric Company (BECO)** as a baseline to map operational performance against fuel consumption overheads.
* **Hybrid Balancing:** Structured system designs to substitute localized decentralized diesel generation units with centralized high-capacity solar-hybrid configurations.
* **Load Management:** Formulated dynamic **Demand-Side Management (DSM)** strategies to distribute industrial peak loads efficiently, stabilizing solar output intermittency issues while lowering overall per-kWh costs.
