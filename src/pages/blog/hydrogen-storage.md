### Institution: University of Tuscia
**Program:** SKILLBILL European Specialization Master | **Timeline:** Sep 2024 - Present

#### Research Focus
Conducted analytical design and transient evaluations investigating localized solid metal hydride and compressed gas storage tanks subjected to dynamic refueling loops.

#### Key Investigated Parameters
* **Pressure Variances:** Evaluated safety boundaries, gas velocities, and thermodynamic dissipation characteristics between **H70 (700 bar)** and **H35 (350 bar)** structural classifications.
* **Intermittency Optimization:** Modeled clean energy configurations balancing alternative storage mediums alongside Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) to support localized grid stability.
* **Application Mapping:** Generated engineering designs linking hydrogen compression mechanisms to store surplus off-peak energy produced by a conceptual 300 kWp photovoltaic infrastructure facility.
